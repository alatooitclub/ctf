About Us


IT CLUB CTF represents dynamic community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to exploring and advancing cybersecurity skills. We organize Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges that provide hands-on experience in solving real-world security problems. Through collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing, we aim to empower students to excel in the world of cybersecurity and beyond

About CTF

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a special kind of information security competitions Teams of competitors (or just individuals) are pitted against each other in a test of computer security skill. Very often CTFs are the beginning of one's cybersecurity career due to their team building nature and competetive aspect. In addition, there isn't a lot of commitment required beyond a weekend.
